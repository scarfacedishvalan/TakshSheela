# Fault Brainstorm: {spec_id}

*This is a Level 1 artifact — completely free-form. No schema. No required structure.
The goal is to generate a wide space of fault ideas before narrowing to candidates.
Quantity and imagination first. Classification comes later.*

---

## System Reading

*In your own words, what is this system and where are its weak points?
What would break first under stress? What would be hardest to debug?*

---

## Fault Ideas

*List every fault idea that comes to mind. Don't classify yet. Don't self-edit.
If an idea feels too obvious or too obscure, write it down anyway.*

---

## Interesting Tensions

*Are there any design tensions in the blueprint that could produce subtle faults?
(e.g. "the worker retries but the queue doesn't deduplicate — what happens?")*

---

## What Would Fool a Strong Engineer

*What faults would a senior engineer take 30+ minutes to find? Why?*

---

## Observability Gaps

*What faults would be invisible or nearly invisible given the current observability level?
Are any of those interesting?*

---

## Candidate Experience Notes

*What would it feel like to be a candidate in this scenario? What's the first thing
they'd check? Where would they waste time? What would make the "aha" moment satisfying?*
